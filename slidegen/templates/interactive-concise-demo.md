## The CLEAR Framework

<div class="key-point">
Context + Logic + Expectation + Action + Response = CLEAR
</div>

### Communication Structure
- **Context** - Why this matters now
- **Logic** - The reasoning behind decisions
- **Expectation** - What success looks like

  <button class="reveal-trigger">

## Response Time Protocols

### The 24-8-2 Rule
- **24 hours** - Acknowledge receipt of all messages
- **8 hours** - Provide initial response during work hours
- **2 hours** - Reply to urgent items (clearly marked)

### Urgency Indicators
- **üî• URGENT** - Response needed within 2 hours
- **‚è∞ TIME-SENSITIVE** - Response needed same day
- **üí≠ FEEDBACK WELCOME** - Response within 24-48 hours

### Status Broadcasting
- **Available** - Normal response times apply
- **Focus Time** - Delayed responses expected
- **Offline** - Emergency contact only
