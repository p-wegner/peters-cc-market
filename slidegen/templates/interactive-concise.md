## Interactive Elements Toolkit

### Micro-Polls
- **Quick opinion checks** - 2-3 options max
- **Real-time feedback** - Instant results
- **Audience segmentation** - Tailor follow-up content

### Progressive Reveals
- **Information chunking** - Prevent cognitive overload
- **Controlled pacing** - Match audience readiness
- **Curiosity building** - Maintain engagement

### Quick Quizzes
- **Knowledge checks** - Validate understanding
- **Active participation** - Break passive consumption
- **Immediate feedback** - Reinforce learning
