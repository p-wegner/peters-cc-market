# Skill Workflow Examples

This document provides real-world examples of how to combine slidegen skills to solve complex presentation challenges.

## Workflow 1: Executive Board Presentation

### Scenario
You need to create a quarterly business review presentation for your company's board of directors.

### Requirements
- Professional corporate styling with company branding
- Current market data and competitive analysis
- Financial performance metrics and KPIs
- Strategic recommendations and next steps
- 15-20 slides for 45-minute presentation

### Skill Combination Workflow

#### Step 1: Research Gathering (Research Presenter)
**Request**: "Gather current market data and competitive analysis for Q3 2024 board presentation. Focus on our industry trends, competitor performance, and market growth projections."

**Output**:
```markdown
## Research Findings for Q3 Board Presentation

### Market Overview
- Industry growth: 8.3% YoY (compared to 6.5% last quarter)
- Market size: $45.2B globally, $12.7B in our region
- Growth drivers: Digital transformation, regulatory changes, consumer behavior shifts

### Competitive Analysis
- Top 3 competitors: Company A (22% market share), Company B (18%), Company C (15%)
- Our position: #4 with 12% market share, up from 10% last year
- Competitive threats: New entrant with innovative technology, price pressure from market leaders

### Financial Benchmarks
- Industry average revenue growth: 12.3%
- Industry average profit margin: 18.7%
- Our performance: 15.2% growth, 21.3% margin (outperforming on profitability)

*Sources: Industry reports, SEC filings, market research databases*
```

#### Step 2: Template Customization (Template Customizer)
**Request**: "Create a corporate template for our board presentations using our brand colors (navy #1e3a8a, gold #b45309), company logo, and executive-level styling. Include financial metric displays and strategic framework layouts."

**Output**: Custom corporate template with:
- Navy and gold color scheme
- Company logo integration in headers
- Financial metric card components
- Strategic framework layouts
- Executive-appropriate typography

#### Step 3: Presentation Creation (Presentation Creator)
**Request**: "Create a 20-slide board presentation using the corporate template and research data. Focus on Q3 performance, market analysis, strategic initiatives, and financial outlook. Include interactive elements for the Q&A session."

**Output**: Complete presentation with:
- Professional title slide with company branding
- Executive summary with key highlights
- Detailed financial performance sections
- Market analysis with competitive positioning
- Strategic initiatives and progress updates
- Financial outlook and projections
- Interactive Q&A preparation slides

#### Step 4: Enhancement and Polish (Slide Enhancer)
**Request**: "Review and enhance the board presentation for maximum executive impact. Focus on clarity of strategic messages, visual hierarchy of financial data, and professional polish."

**Final Output**: Polished presentation ready for board delivery

### Workflow Benefits
- **Credibility**: Research-backed with current market data
- **Professionalism**: Consistent corporate branding throughout
- **Clarity**: Well-structured narrative with clear takeaways
- **Impact**: Engaging visuals and strategic messaging

## General Best Practices for Skill Workflows

### Planning Your Workflow

#### 1. Identify Your Primary Need
- **New content needed**: Start with Presentation Creator
- **Existing content improvement**: Start with Slide Enhancer
- **Research required**: Start with Research Presenter
- **Branding needed**: Start with Template Customizer

#### 2. Consider Dependencies
- Research should inform content creation
- Template customization should precede content creation
- Enhancement is typically the final step
- Multiple iterations may be needed

#### 3. Plan for Integration
- Ensure consistent messaging across skills
- Maintain brand standards throughout
- Plan for audience appropriateness
- Allow time for refinement and feedback

### Common Workflow Patterns

#### Research-Heavy Presentations
```
Research Presenter → Template Customizer → Presentation Creator → Slide Enhancer
```

#### Brand-Focused Presentations
```
Template Customizer → Presentation Creator → Slide Enhancer
```

#### Improvement Projects
```
Slide Enhancer (may include research integration)
```

#### Technical Presentations
```
Research Presenter → Presentation Creator → Slide Enhancer
```

#### Complex Multi-Phase Projects
```
Research → Template → Content → Enhancement → Review → Final Polish
```

### Success Indicators

#### Workflow Success Metrics
- **Efficiency**: Minimal rework between skills
- **Consistency**: Cohesive final presentation
- **Quality**: Professional output that meets objectives
- **Effectiveness**: Presentation achieves intended outcomes

#### Quality Checkpoints
- After each skill completion, review outputs
- Ensure transitions between skills are smooth
- Validate that requirements are being met
- Plan for final integration and polish

These workflow examples demonstrate how combining slidegen skills can solve complex presentation challenges while maintaining professional quality and consistency throughout the process.
